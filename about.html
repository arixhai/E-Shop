About Us

Welcome to ShopEase, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 1993 by William Smith, ShopEase has come a long way from its beginnings in a small garage next to Williams' house. When William first started out, his passion for quality shopping, drove them to do tons of research, so that ShopEase can offer you the world's most advanced shopping experience. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,
William Smith, Founder

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional online shopping experience while being safe and getting the best of quality out there. We strive to achieve the biggest global online shopping network in the world and changing the way you shop online and we believe in equal opportunities for all, regardless of age, disability, origin or nationality, faith, gender or gender identity, or sexual orientation..

Our Vision

Our vision is to make online shopping easy and safe for everyone all around the world. We aim to give access of the best quality in the whole world so that everyone gets the same online experience wherever they might be and impact on the world.
